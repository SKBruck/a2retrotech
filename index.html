A2 Retro Tech

A2 Retro Tech meetups generally happen on the 3rd Wednesday of the month and are largely unstructured - just a conversation about anything and everything retro: BBSes, Usenet, Apple II, CP/M, MS-DOS, Amiga, C64, etc.
