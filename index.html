A2 Retro Tech

  Due to COVID-19, A2 Retro Tech currently meets online.  

A2 Retro Tech meetups usually happen on the 3rd Wednesday of the month from 7pm - 9pm. In the first hour, we do introductions and chat about all things retro tech. In the second hour, a member performs a demo or tear-down, and we have a more in depth discussion.
